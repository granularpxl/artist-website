Visions of Vactrols was written late in 2013, at which point I was immersed completely in the world of sampling. Many of the tracks began as reincarnations of previous works, sampled and deformed to become something new, with the intention that they too would eventually be recycled. In comparison to my previous release, I consciously focused on using more natural sounds, incorporating stronger grooves and experimenting a lot more.
